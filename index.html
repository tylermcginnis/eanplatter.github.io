growing old

February 28 2014

sometimes growing old isn't about age. sometimes it's about attitude. sometimes growing old is about connection. sometimes you can be old, and gray, and still young. sometimes you can be young, and yet old. does it really matter? was growing up really ever a proper goal? perhaps making connection is more proper. perhaps creating beauty is more important. perhaps finding insight is better than harboring experience. i really can't tell yet. i haven't found enough insight to be sure about most things.
